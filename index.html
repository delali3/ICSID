About ICSSID

Building bridges between research, practice, and policy

Background and Information

The International Conference on Sustainable Science and Infrastructure Development (ICSSID) is an interdisciplinary, double-blind, peer-reviewed event that provides a global platform for advancing research and practice including but not limited to sustainability science, resilient infrastructure, and emerging technologies.

The conference aims to tackle urgent global challenges by integrating scientific research, engineering solutions, and policy frameworks to foster long-term sustainable development.

ICSSID covers three broad thematic areas:

  • Sustainability Science: climate-resilient urban planning, green materials, circular economy, and energy-efficient systems across environmental, economic, and social dimensions.
  • Infrastructure Development: sustainable transport, smart cities, renewable energy systems, infrastructure finance, governance, and inclusive policy approaches.
  • Technical Innovation & Cross-Disciplinary Methods: AI, IoT, smart grids, and digital twin technologies applied to sustainable infrastructure systems.

Proceedings & Indexing

ICSSID proceedings will be published with IEEE and indexed in major databases including IEEE Xplore and Scopus, ensuring wide visibility and impact for presented research. Selected papers will be considered for publication in the Ghana Journal of Technology, Ghana Mining Journal, and Journal of Sustainability Science and Infrastructure (New).

Important details for participants

Publication

Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings and considered for submission to indexed journals including IEEE Xplore and Scopus.

Presentation Format

The conference will feature oral presentations, poster sessions, and panel discussions. Each paper will have a 15-minute presentation slot including Q&A.

Registration

Early bird registration is available until June 15, 2026. Student discounts and group rates are available. Registration includes conference materials and refreshments.

Venue Access

The conference will be held at UMaT-SRID campus in Sekondi-Takoradi, Ghana. Detailed venue information and directions will be provided to registered participants.
