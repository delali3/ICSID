Three Pillars of ICSID

Our conference is structured around three interconnected thematic areas that represent the core domains of sustainable development and technological innovation

Sustainability Science

Climate-resilient urban planning, green materials, circular economy, and energy-efficient systems across environmental, economic, and social dimensions.

Infrastructure Development
