The International Conference on Sustainable Science and Infrastructure Development (ICSID 2026) invites original research papers that address the critical challenges facing our world today. Submission Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines carefully to ensure your submission is considered for review

Paper Types

Full Papers: 6-8 pages (including references)

Short Papers: 3-4 pages (including references)

Poster Papers: 2 pages (including references)

Industry Papers: 4-6 pages (including references)

Format Requirements

Template: IEEE Conference Template

Font: Times New Roman, 10pt

Format: Two-column, single-spaced

File Type: PDF only

File Size: Maximum 10 MB

Review Process

Review Type: Double-blind peer review

Reviewers: Minimum 3 expert reviewers

Criteria: Novelty, technical quality, clarity

Timeline: 6-8 weeks review period

Publication

Venue: IEEE Xplore Digital Library

Indexing: Scopus, Web of Science eligible

DOI: Assigned to all published papers

Copyright: IEEE Copyright form required

Submission Checklist

Paper follows IEEE conference template
All author information removed for blind review
References properly formatted
Figures and tables are high quality
Paper length within specified limits
Plagiarism check completed
